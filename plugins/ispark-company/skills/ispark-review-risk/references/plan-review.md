# Plan Review

Challenge:

- Is the stated outcome the real objective?
- Is the smallest useful version clear?
- What user or operator behavior proves success?
- What data/contract boundary could break?
- Is a proposed simplification hiding future rework?
- Are there cheaper ways to learn before building?

For founder/product review, push on demand, differentiation, workflow fit, and hidden distribution cost.

For engineering review, push on ownership boundaries, rollout plan, rollback, observability, tests, and migration safety.
