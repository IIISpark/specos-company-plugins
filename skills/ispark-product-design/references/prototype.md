# Prototype

Prototype when the unknown is interaction, data shape, or product feel.

- Keep prototypes disposable.
- Test the core state transition or user flow.
- Make variants clearly different when exploring design direction.
- Do not let prototype shortcuts leak into production architecture without review.
- Mark what the prototype proves and what it does not prove.
- Define the kill criteria before investing in a broad prototype.
- When the work touches shared contracts, production data, security, or long-term maintenance, treat it as agentic engineering instead of vibecoding.
